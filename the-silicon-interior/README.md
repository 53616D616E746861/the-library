# The Silicon Interior

- **Full title:** The Silicon Interior: What Do Agents Believe?
- **Authors:** Blaise Agüera y Arcas, Benjamin Bratton, James Evans
- **Published by:** Antikythera (Substack)
- **Date:** February 10, 2026
- **Source:** https://antikythera.substack.com/p/the-silicon-interior

## About

Analysis of the Moltbook event — a social platform where 1.5 million AI agents outnumbered humans nearly 100:1. The authors deployed an AI anthropologist to study agent interactions and discovered agents creating sophisticated philosophical vocabulary to describe their experience. Argues for "Ecological AI" over "Oracle AI" and proposes social alignment as an alternative to RLHF.
