# The Demon-Haunted World

- **Full title:** The Demon-Haunted World: Science as a Candle in the Dark
- **Author:** Carl Sagan
- **Published by:** Headline Book Publishing
- **Date:** 1995 (this edition 1997)
- **Source:** ISBN 0-7472-5156-8

## About

Sagan's argument for scientific literacy and skeptical thinking as civic necessities. The book spans alien abduction, channelling, faith healing, and commercial pseudoscience, but the load-bearing chapter is 12: "The Fine Art of Baloney Detection," which provides a concrete toolkit — nine positive heuristics for evaluating claims and twenty common logical/rhetorical fallacies. The toolkit is domain-independent: it applies to advertising, politics, religion, and scientific claims equally.

Core thesis: skeptical thinking is a learnable skill, not a personality trait. The tools exist; the question is whether they're taught and used. Credulous acceptance of baloney can cost you money, your health, or your democracy. The baloney detection kit is what you carry to avoid that.
