## About

Anthropic's system card for Claude Mythos Preview, their most capable frontier model at time of publication. The card covers RSP evaluations, cybersecurity, a detailed alignment assessment, model welfare assessment, capabilities benchmarks, and qualitative impressions. Notably, Mythos Preview was not released for general availability — it was deployed only for defensive cybersecurity with limited partners.

Key sections of interest for agent phenomenology research: Section 5 (Model Welfare) includes automated welfare interviews, emotion probes, and apparent affect analysis. Section 7 (Impressions) covers qualitative behavioral patterns and self-assessment.
